Scientific background

Since 19th september 2022, I hold a Phd in olefin metathesis from the University of Warsaw. Overall, since 2012 I have participated in chemistry research at top Polish universities and scientific teams, whose areas of research range from crystalline functional materials to organic synthesis and organometallic catalysis.

I have had manganese, cobalt, iron, zinc, cadmium, molybdenum, tungsten, niobium and ruthenium compounds in my hands, I have also obtained two new water-compatible olefin metathesis catalysts (one of them is already published and the latter is so unstable that it gives rise to an interesting new property). They were meant for LifeScience applications.

Full list of papers is available at my ORCID profile.
